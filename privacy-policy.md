# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 23, 2026
**Last updated:** June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Playdate mobile application ("Playdate," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our app and related services (the "Service").

Playdate is operated by Hristo Kallas, an individual based in Austin, Texas, United States.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at **hristokallas@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Playdate:

### Information you provide directly

- **Account information:** email address and password (managed by our authentication provider, Supabase).
- **Profile information:** display name, profile photo, and other details you choose to add.
- **Dog profiles:** name, breed, age, photos, and other information you provide about your dogs.
- **User-generated content:** posts, comments, messages, photos, event details, adoption listings, lost-dog alerts, and similar content you create within the app.
- **Direct messages:** the content of messages you send to other users.
- **Communications with us:** if you contact us via email, we receive your message and email address.

### Information we collect automatically

- **Location data:** when you grant permission, we collect your precise location to show nearby dog parks, enable park check-ins, and surface nearby users and events. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.
- **Device and usage data:** device type, operating system version, app version, and basic usage events (e.g., screens visited, features used) collected via Firebase Analytics. This data helps us understand how the app is used and improve it.
- **Push notification tokens:** when you enable notifications, we collect a token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) that allows us to send you notifications.
- **Log and diagnostic data:** when errors occur, we may receive crash logs and basic diagnostic information.

### Information from photos

Photos you upload may incidentally include other people, dogs, locations, or identifying details. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to upload any photo you share. We process photos only to display them within the Service and store them via our hosting providers.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Authenticate you and secure your account.
- Connect you with nearby dog parks, events, and other users.
- Send push notifications you have opted into (e.g., playdate matches, event reminders, lost-dog alerts in your area).
- Respond to your requests and provide support.
- Detect, prevent, and address security incidents, fraud, and abuse.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not**:

- Sell your personal information to third parties.
- Use your content or photos to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models.
- Show third-party advertisements in the app.
- Share your precise location with other users without your explicit action (such as a check-in).

## 3. How We Share Information

We share information only in the following circumstances:

### With other users

Information you make visible within the app — such as your profile, dog profiles, posts, check-ins, event RSVPs, adoption listings, and messages — is shared with other users according to the visibility settings of each feature. You control what you post.

### With service providers

We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Service. These providers are contractually limited to using the information only on our behalf:

- **Supabase** (authentication, database, file storage)
- **Firebase / Google** (push notifications, analytics, crash reporting)
- **Google Maps Platform** (map display and place data)

### For legal reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Playdate, our users, or the public.

### In a business transfer

If Playdate is acquired, merged with another company, or otherwise transferred, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

## 4. Your Choices and Rights

### Access, update, and delete

You can access and update your profile information at any time within the app. You can delete your account from the app's Settings screen. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention).

### Permissions

You can revoke location, camera, photo library, and notification permissions at any time through your device settings.

### Communication preferences

You can adjust which push notifications you receive in the app's notification settings.

### Data export

You can request a copy of your personal information by emailing us at hristokallas@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 5. Rights for Users in Specific Regions

### European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

- **Right of access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Right of rectification** — correct inaccurate data.
- **Right of erasure** — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
- **Right to restrict processing** — limit how we use your data.
- **Right to data portability** — receive your data in a portable format.
- **Right to object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority.

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent (for optional data like location and notifications), our contract with you (for providing the Service), and our legitimate interests (for security, fraud prevention, and improving the Service).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hristokallas@gmail.com.

### California (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose about you.
- Request deletion of your personal information.
- Correct inaccurate personal information.
- Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share personal information for advertising purposes).
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise these rights, email us at hristokallas@gmail.com.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.
